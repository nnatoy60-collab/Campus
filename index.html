About ZDSPGC Dimataling Campus

Our Mission

ZDSPGC Dimataling Campus is committed to providing accessible, quality, and relevant education that transforms students into competent and ethical professionals ready to meet the challenges of the global community.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution that produces graduates who are catalysts for positive change in society through excellence in instruction, research, and community engagement.

Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world. - Nelson Mandela
